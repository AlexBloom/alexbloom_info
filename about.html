About

I'm Alex Bloom. I am an identity and interaction designer. I teach design as an adjunct professor at the Tyler School of Art and I spend a great deal of time outdoors. It is my intent to leave the world having improved it with my work.

I currently work at Bluecadet Interactive as an interaction designer and developer working on a mix of websites, apps, and exhibitions for museums, non-profits, and arts & cultural institutions.

I've been called a perfectionist. I'm currently accepting select freelance projects. If you would like to discuss a project, or just want to say hello, please feel free to get in touch.
